Club Shub is a fine establishment where monsters go to loosen up after a hard
day of guarding the castles and military bases of Quake. If this is your
first night at Club Shub, just hide in a dark corner or stand on the upper
deck and watch the ruckus! It may take you a while to get your courage up,
but you'll want to get down in the pit and mosh like a madman before the
night is over. Things may get a litle rowdy at ClubShub, but fighting is
discouraged. Please do NOT shoot the patrons - take out your aggression in
the pit. If any monsters die in club shub, another monster will come in
through one of the front doors. This ensures that there will be plenty of
action in the pit all night long. When you first start, most monsters will
just be thrashing in the pit. Just give them some time, they'll get real
wild.

Basically, we were REAL frickin bored on Christmas day, and we somehow decided
that we wanted to make a monster mosh pit and give it to you guys as a cheesy
Christmas present. There's some really nice dynamic behavior on the part of
the monsters. They mosh, run, stage dive, even crowd surf if they can land
on anyone! There's an extra weapon in Club Shub (it's not very well hidden)
but you still have to find it. Impulse 9 will not give you the new weapon,
impulse 20 will activate the weapon when you find it. The new weapon is an
old patch that has been lying around on my hard drive (with about 15 other
never-before-seen patches) and John pestered me to put it into this add on, so
I did. :)  When you first start out in Club Shub, don't shoot monsters. They
are here to Mosh, not fight. No one will attack you, and if you get in a
fight, you'll only have to deal with the guy you picked a fight with- the rest
of the patrons will not jump you. It's a blast to watch the monsters get down
and funky. Wait till you see an ogre crowd surfing.

This patch was made in a day, guys. There are tons of features we wanted to
add, but we decided to just finish up the features that were already in and
release. I'm not very proud of the code I wrote for this project... it works,
but it's pretty sloppy. :(  There are tons of little tweaks that I wanted
to add to the animation sequences, but we've spent enough time on this one
for now. To the best of my knowledge, all bugs are gone. The source will not
be released.


Known Problems:
---------------
-A couple fullbright pixels on the new model
-Don't try to play normal QUAKE maps with the CLUBSHUB patch. :)


Installation/Use:
-----------------
Make a directory off of your quake directory called 'clubshub'

copy the pak0.pak file from this archive into the clubshub directory

type 'quake -game clubshub +map clubshub' to begin.


That's it. Merry Christmas, hope you like.
